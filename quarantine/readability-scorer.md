# readability-scorer

Flesch-Kincaid readability scorer with grade level, reading ease, and improvement suggestions.

## Requirements
- fleschEase(text): Flesch Reading Ease score 0-100
- fleschKincaidGrade(text): grade level
- gunningFog(text): Gunning Fog index
- analyze(text): all metrics with descriptive labels (Easy, Difficult, etc.)
- suggestions(text): actionable improvement tips based on scores
