# kinetic-potential-energy

Convert between kinetic and potential energy and calculate total mechanical energy in a system.

## Requirements
- KE = 0.5mv^2, PE = mgh
- Total mechanical energy and conservation check
- Velocity from height (energy conservation)
- Height from velocity (energy conservation)
