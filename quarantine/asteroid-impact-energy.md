# asteroid-impact-energy

Estimate kinetic energy, crater diameter, and equivalent TNT for asteroid impacts.

## Requirements
- KE = 0.5*m*v^2, convert to megatons TNT
- Pi-group scaling laws for crater diameter
- Atmospheric entry and airburst altitude estimate
- Tunguska and Chicxulub as reference benchmarks
