# schwarzschild-radius

Calculate Schwarzschild radius, event horizon properties, and Hawking temperature for black holes.

## Requirements
- Rs = 2GM/c^2
- Hawking temperature T = hbar*c^3/(8*pi*G*M*k_B)
- Evaporation time estimate
- Tidal force at event horizon
