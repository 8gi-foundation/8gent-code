# pendulum-simulator

Simulate simple and physical pendulum motion, returning period and angular frequency.

## Requirements
- Simple pendulum: period from length and gravity
- Physical pendulum: period from moment of inertia, mass, and pivot distance
- Support small-angle and large-angle (elliptic integral approximation) modes
- Return period, frequency, and angular frequency
