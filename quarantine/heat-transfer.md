# heat-transfer

Calculate conductive, convective, and radiative heat transfer rates.

## Requirements
- Conduction: Fourier's law (k, area, delta-T, thickness)
- Convection: Newton's law (h, area, delta-T)
- Radiation: Stefan-Boltzmann (emissivity, area, T^4 difference)
- Return heat flux in watts
