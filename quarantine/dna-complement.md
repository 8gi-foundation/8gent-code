# dna-complement

Generate DNA complement, reverse complement, RNA transcript, and GC content from a sequence.

## Requirements
- DNA complement: A-T, G-C pairing
- Reverse complement for anti-parallel strand
- DNA to mRNA transcription (T->U)
- Return GC content and sequence length
