# pluralize

English pluralization and singularization with irregular word support.

## Requirements
- pluralize(word, count?) returns plural or singular based on count
- singularize(word) returns singular form
- Built-in irregulars: person/people, child/children, etc.
- Built-in uncountables: information, series, etc.
- addIrregular(singular, plural) for custom words
