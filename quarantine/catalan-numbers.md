# catalan-numbers

Compute Catalan numbers and enumerate the combinatorial structures they count.

## Requirements
- C_n = C(2n,n)/(n+1) with BigInt support
- Applications: Dyck paths, balanced parentheses, triangulations
- Generate all balanced parenthesis strings for C_n
- Asymptotic approximation for large n
