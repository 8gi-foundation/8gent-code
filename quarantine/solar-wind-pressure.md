# solar-wind-pressure

Estimate solar wind dynamic pressure, magnetopause standoff distance, and planetary bow shock.

## Requirements
- Dynamic pressure P = 0.5 * n * m_p * v^2
- Magnetopause standoff: pressure balance with planetary dipole field
- Typical solar wind parameters at 1 AU
- Scale with heliocentric distance
