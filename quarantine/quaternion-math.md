# quaternion-math

Perform quaternion arithmetic, rotation operations, and SLERP interpolation.

## Requirements
- Quaternion multiply, conjugate, norm, inverse
- Rotation quaternion from axis-angle and Euler angles
- Apply rotation to 3D vector
- SLERP between two rotation quaternions
