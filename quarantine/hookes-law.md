# hookes-law

Apply Hooke's law to calculate spring force, extension, elastic potential energy, and oscillation period.

## Requirements
- F = -kx for force from displacement
- Elastic PE = 0.5kx^2
- SHM period T = 2pi*sqrt(m/k)
- Return force, energy, and period given spring constant, mass, displacement
