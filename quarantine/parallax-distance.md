# parallax-distance

Convert stellar parallax angle to distance in parsecs, light-years, and AU.

## Requirements
- d(pc) = 1/p(arcsec)
- Convert arcseconds, milliarcseconds, microarcseconds
- Return distance in pc, ly, and AU
- Proper motion to transverse velocity conversion
