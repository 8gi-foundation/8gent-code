# partnership-scorecard

Evaluates and scores potential business partnerships across strategic fit, reach, and risk.

## Requirements
- scorePartner({ name, audienceOverlap, techCompatibility, brandAlignment, risk }): returns composite score
- rank(partners[]): returns sorted list by score
- renderScorecard(partner): formatted partner evaluation card
- comparePartners(a, b): side-by-side dimension comparison
