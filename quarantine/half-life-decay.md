# half-life-decay

Calculate radioactive and pharmacological half-life decay with dose scheduling.

## Requirements
- N(t) = N0*(1/2)^(t/t_half)
- Time to reach target fraction
- Multi-dose accumulation (trough/peak)
- Biological, radioactive, and effective half-life combination
