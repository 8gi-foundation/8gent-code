# gravitational-wave-freq

Estimate gravitational wave frequency and strain from binary inspiral systems.

## Requirements
- Orbital frequency and GW frequency (2x orbital)
- Chirp mass from component masses
- Strain amplitude h at given distance
- Time to merger from orbital separation (Peters formula)
