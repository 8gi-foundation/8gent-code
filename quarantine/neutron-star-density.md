# neutron-star-density

Calculate neutron star properties: density, surface gravity, escape velocity, and rotation limits.

## Requirements
- Typical NS: M ~1.4 Msun, R ~10 km
- Central density vs nuclear density ratio
- Surface gravity and escape velocity
- Breakup rotation rate (equatorial velocity = orbital velocity)
