# stefan-boltzmann

Calculate blackbody radiation power, peak wavelength (Wien's law), and luminosity.

## Requirements
- Total power P = epsilon*sigma*A*T^4
- Wien's displacement: lambda_max = b/T
- Planck spectral radiance at given wavelength and temperature
- Stellar luminosity from radius and temperature
