# hashtag-strategist

Hashtag strategy builder for social media: extracts themes, categorizes by reach, and builds sets.

## Requirements
- extract(text): derives hashtag candidates from content keywords
- categorize(hashtags[], volumes{}): niche | medium | large by volume ranges
- buildSet(content, { niche, medium, broad }): balanced hashtag set
- renderStrategy(set): formatted hashtag strategy with rationale
