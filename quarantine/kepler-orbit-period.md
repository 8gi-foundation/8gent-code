# kepler-orbit-period

Apply Kepler's laws to compute orbital period, semi-major axis, and orbital speed.

## Requirements
- Third law: T^2 = 4*pi^2*a^3/GM
- Vis-viva equation for speed at any point
- Ellipse parameters from periapsis and apoapsis
- Synodic period from two orbital periods
