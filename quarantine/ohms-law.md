# ohms-law

Solve Ohm's law for any missing variable given two of voltage, current, and resistance.

## Requirements
- Accept any two of V, I, R and solve for the third
- Calculate power (P=VI) as a bonus output
- Handle parallel and series resistance combinations
- Return all quantities with units
