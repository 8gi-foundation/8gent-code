# hohmann-transfer

Calculate delta-v requirements and transfer time for Hohmann orbital transfer maneuvers.

## Requirements
- Two-burn delta-v from initial and target circular orbits
- Transfer time (half ellipse period)
- Bi-elliptic transfer comparison for large radius ratios
- Return delta-v budget and total transfer time
