# ph-calculator

Calculate pH, pOH, [H+], and [OH-] for strong acids, bases, and buffer solutions.

## Requirements
- Strong acid/base: pH from concentration
- Weak acid: pH from Ka and concentration (quadratic)
- Henderson-Hasselbalch for buffers
- Convert between pH, pOH, [H+], [OH-]
