# reaction-rate

Calculate reaction rate, rate constant from Arrhenius equation, and half-life for various orders.

## Requirements
- Zero, first, second order integrated rate laws
- Arrhenius: k = A*exp(-Ea/RT)
- Half-life for each order
- Activation energy from two-temperature rate constants
