# osmotic-pressure

Calculate osmotic pressure, tonicity, and colligative properties of solutions.

## Requirements
- van 't Hoff: pi = iMRT
- Boiling point elevation and freezing point depression
- Osmolarity from ionic dissociation
- Isotonic, hypotonic, hypertonic classification
