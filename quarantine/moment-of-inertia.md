# moment-of-inertia

Calculate moment of inertia for common geometric shapes about standard axes.

## Requirements
- Solid sphere, hollow sphere, solid cylinder, hollow cylinder, thin rod, rectangular plate
- Accept mass and relevant dimensions
- Return moment of inertia in kg*m^2
- Apply parallel axis theorem for offset axes
