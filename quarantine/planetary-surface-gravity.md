# planetary-surface-gravity

Calculate surface gravity, weight on other worlds, and gravitational acceleration at altitude.

## Requirements
- g = GM/R^2
- Weight on all solar system bodies as multiplier table
- g at altitude h: g(h) = g0*(R/(R+h))^2
- Equatorial gravity reduction due to rotation
