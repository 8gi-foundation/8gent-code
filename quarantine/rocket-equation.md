# rocket-equation

Apply Tsiolkovsky rocket equation to calculate delta-v, mass ratio, and propellant requirements.

## Requirements
- delta_v = v_e * ln(m0/m_f)
- Mass ratio from target delta-v and exhaust velocity
- Multi-stage rocket delta-v summation
- Specific impulse (Isp) to exhaust velocity conversion
