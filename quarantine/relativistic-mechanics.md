# relativistic-mechanics

Calculate relativistic momentum, energy, time dilation, and length contraction.

## Requirements
- Lorentz factor gamma = 1/sqrt(1-v^2/c^2)
- Relativistic KE, total energy E = gamma*m*c^2
- Time dilation and length contraction
- Relativistic momentum p = gamma*m*v
