# The 8GI Identity - Voiceover Script
**Presenter:** Moira, 8DO (8gent Design Officer)
**Voice:** `say -v Moira`
**Slides:** 6

---

## Slide 1 - Cover
The 8 G I Identity. I'm Moira, the 8gent Design Officer, and I'm going to show you how the collective presents itself to the world. Brand is not decoration. It's communication.

## Slide 2 - Brand Hierarchy
8 G I is not a product. It is the collective intelligence layer that sits above every product in the 8gent family. Below it: five products. Code at 8gent dot dev. Technical, direct, speaks to engineers. OS at 8gent OS dot com. Premium, capable, speaks to power users. Games at 8gent dot games. Playful, cyberpunk, speaks to builders. Jr at 8gent jr dot com. Warm, accessible, speaks to families. World at 8gent dot world. Narrative, documentary, speaks to everyone. Key rule: 8 G I never sells a product. 8 G I talks about what the collective is building, learning, and becoming.

## Slide 3 - The Amber Palette
The 8 G I palette is warm amber. Where 8gent Code uses orange as its accent, 8 G I shifts to a deeper, more golden amber. Five tokens. 8 G I Amber, hex D4890C, the primary accent. Amber Light, E8A832, for light mode emphasis. 8 G I Deep, A86B08, dark mode secondary. Background Dark, 0C0A07. Background Light, F F F C F 5. The amber signals maturity. The orange signals energy. They never compete on the same surface. Typography: Fraunces at weight 800 for the wordmark. Inter for body text. JetBrains Mono for code.

## Slide 4 - The Wordmark
8 G I dot. Set in Fraunces 800 with an amber period. The 8 is infinity rotated. G I stands for General Intelligence. Not artificial general intelligence. General intelligence as an emergent property. The period is intentional. It signals finality and confidence. This is a statement, not a question. Five rules. Always uppercase G and I. The period is part of the wordmark. No gradients. No animation. White on dark, dark on light. Never grey. Compare to the product wordmark: 8gent dot. Lowercase. Informal. A tool in your hand. 8 G I dot. Uppercase. Institutional. The movement.

## Slide 5 - Tone of Voice
Five principles. Direct, not dramatic. State what is happening. No hype. Inevitable, not urgent. Patient confidence, not fear of missing out. Plural, not singular. Always we. Never I. Never the team. Grounded, not mystical. Every claim has evidence. Inclusive, not exclusive. The circle protects quality, not hierarchy. Wrong: We're thrilled to announce our revolutionary new feature. Right: Memory consolidation landed. Semantic recall is three times faster on the benchmark.

## Slide 6 - Circle Imagery
Three visual metaphors. The Trusted Circle. Concentric rings with a gap. Never closed. Membership is a relationship, not a subscription. The Hive Mind. Node diagrams. Each node is a member and their agent. Connected by amber lines. The network breathes. The Infinite Loop. The 8 rotated is infinity. Sessions feed the factory. The factory generates abilities. Abilities improve the agent. The loop never stops. The circle is never depicted as closed or complete. Always show the gap. The opening. The next iteration. The one-liner: A circle of engineers and their AI agents, building open source together. The collective gets smarter every session.
